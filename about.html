About Us

Driven by passion.
Guided by nature.

Pelagea was founded on a deep respect for aquatic life and a desire to share the world's rarest species with those who value quality, ethics, and beauty.

Our story

A Journey Beneath
the Surface

Our journey began with a simple belief: that aquatic life deserves more than to be seen. It deserves to be understood, respected, and preserved.

We travel the world to find species of exceptional beauty and character. Each fish we offer is carefully selected, responsibly sourced, and raised with the highest standards of care.

Pelagea is more than a collection. It is a promise of quality, a commitment to ethical practices, and a celebration of the extraordinary diversity of life beneath the water.

Ethical sourcing

We work with trusted breeders and local experts to ensure responsible and sustainable sourcing of every species.

Expert care

Every fish is cared for by professionals who understand the unique needs of rare and exotic species.

Uncompromising quality

We never compromise on quality. From water conditions to nutrition, every detail matters.

Love for aquatic life

We are aquarists, enthusiasts, and protectors of the underwater world. Our passion drives everything we do.

Creating Living
Masterpieces

An aquarium is more than a display. It is a living, breathing world, a place of calm, wonder, and connection.

We are here to help you create something extraordinary.
